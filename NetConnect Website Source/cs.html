Using Customers To Drive Better Customer Experience. Win-Win.

The Power of Crowd Sourced Analytics

In the UK more than two thirds of people own smartphones, these have now overtaken laptops as the primary way of getting online. As a result, people typically carry their smartphones with them providing a constant connection to the network. 5App’s NetConnect and NetConnect Library allow Network Operator’s to mine crowd sourced information from their customers, with smartphones acting like millions of network probes.

Importantly, NetConnect analyses device and network data that is anonymised and does not capture personal information. The crowd sourced analytics provides a constantly updated view of network coverage and signal strength. This is beneficial, since it is measured rather predicted and therefore provides a more accurate coverage map. Since the data is constantly being collected, the impact of network optimisation and NRO activities can be quickly be evaluated to see if they have had the desired outcome.

However, NetConnect provides more than just crowd sourced coverage data. The solution informs the customer about device and mobile network issues specific to them. Allowing fully automated updates that keep them informed of the status of the network, such as planned/unplanned outages, network performance and much more.

Smartphones are such a well adopted technology that they provide a great source of data to gain insights into large scale human behavior. NetConnect can therefore be used for non-telecommunication crowd sourced application and the NetConnect Library can be incorporated into third-party applications to act as a human activity sensor.
