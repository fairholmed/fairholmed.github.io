About Us

We are 5App.

5App is the telecoms and networking division of 5app Limited.

At 5app, we love simple and straightforward. Our goal is to help Network Operators and Network Enablers gain unique insights into their subscribers, networks and services and Mobile Subscribers gain the most from their subscriptions.

We are a young company but with a history steeped in the mobile world and we want to help organisations embrace the benefits of 5App technology to drive customer satisfaction.

We combine a deep understanding of mobile and network analytics with robust, intuitive, simple-to-use mobile apps and software. We offer solutions that integrate easily into existing tool sets and quickly provide a ROI.

Simple & Straightforward

Design and usability are central to our apps and software. Everything we build is simple and straight forward to use.

Flexible & Adaptive

Our mobile apps are built for Android and iOS mobile phones and tablets. Whilst our backend solutions are vendor agnostic, allowing flexible integration with multiple OSSs and other systems.

Rapid Payback

Our enterprise solutions have low installation and on-going operational costs, but provide access to highly valuable business intelligence and unlock new revenue streams.
