Customer Experience Management

How likely would you be to recommend Your Mobile Operator to friends or family?

How 5App Can Help With CEM

Technology users have ever increasing expectations when it comes to customer service. For a mobile customer this desire includes:

  • A more personalised user experience that reflects their location, their connectivity, their device and their usage
  • Full transparency of network problems, poor or degraded coverage and planned work
  • To be always connected, with the ability to raise issues immediately 24/7, to get an immediate response and frequent updates to their issues

As social media makes it easier for dis-satisfied customer to vent their anger and be heard by an increased audience, the value of promoters is increasing. It is imperative that network operators prevent customers from becoming detractors and whenever possible create new promoters. The 5App tool suite allows Network Operators to continuously measure customer experience from a network subscriber and mobile device perspective and to meet their ever increasing expectations. Here are some of the ways we facilitate better customer service, make customer experience management easier and help improve operators' Net Promoter Scores.

Reduced Call Centre Load

The NetConnect client app enables users to raise issues directly. This reduces call loads, which has a direct cost saving, to operators. However, since an ever increasing number of users prefer this mode of interaction this is seen as a positive. A win-win.

Improved Response Time

When a user raises an issue in real time, using the NetConnect app, the ticket is enriched with device and network data. This assists engineers in performing root cause analysis and there increases the speed and probability of providing the customer with a fix.

 

Self Help

Users love it when they can solve their own issues, quickly, painlessly and without calling customer care. The NetConnect app has a number of self-diagnostic features and an interactive trouble shooting function that enables the user to resolve an array of device issues.

 

Information Transparency

Customers feel reassured when they know that their provider is aware of their issues and actively trying to resolve them. The NetConnect app enables the operator to provide users with advanced notifications of outages and updates for on-going incidents, based on their location.

Avoid The Wrong Conclusions

When a mobile user cannot reach a web site or their app isn't working correctly, they can be inclined to blame their network service provider. NetConnect helps the customer distinguish between network and other issues and stops them jumping to the wrong conclusion.
