HomeSurveyor

In-building mobile network coverage can be very different from the predicted coverage that a Network Operator advertises. Homesurveyor allows you to create an informative report, that helps you make the important decisions.

Moving House

When it comes to moving house, mobile signal coverage is now deemed as important as school catchment areas and access to local amenities.

We think mobile signal strength should be part of any estate agent pack or home survey report. With 5App's HomeSurveyor app it can be.

Use the app to take measurements in the house and we will provide you with an expert report detailing the mobile experience you will get.

Switching Mobile Provider

If your mobile contract is coming to an end, you may decide moving to a new network provider makes better financial sense, they have a wider range of handsets or they provide better customer service. Whatever the reason, before you switch you should compare the network coverage at home.

Use 5App's HomeSurveyor app to measure different operators coverage and we will provide a report telling you what to expect from each provider.

Ditching Fixed Line

With super-fast 4G already rolled out across much of the UK (and available in most countries), mobile broadband now provides a viable alternative to ADSL over fixed line or fiber. There are many advantages to this approach: mobile broadband is portable and more flexible, you can save money consolidating services, you do not need a fixed line and installation is simpler.

However, before choosing to go ahead we recommend that you check that your indoor mobile coverage is adequate and that you compare network providers. Download the HomeSurveyor app, take some measurements and we will give you our expert opinion.

Download the HomeSurveyor App Today

Download the HomeSurveyor app today, from the Google Play Store. (Apple iPhone version coming soon).

Using the app is very simple, fill in some information about you and the property and then take some measurements, using the app. Use different SIMs or different devices to take measurements on different networks. Then press the "Report" button and we will analyse the data and email you a detailed report. The report will explain what the indoor mobile coverage looks like at the property, what your user experience is likely to be and our recommendations.
